Stage 1: Die rolls

  • You will roll a die {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} times.
  • After each roll, you report the number you rolled.
  • The number you report is the number of points you will be paid for that roll.
  • 1 point is worth {{ C.conversion }}.

Stage 3: Extra interaction with a participant from another study

  • After stage 3, you are matched with a new participant from a different study.
  • The other participant will now see only one of your randomly selected die roll, as well as the number you reported for that roll.
  • Based on this information, they will have the opportunity to send points to them.
  • Every point they send is tripled: 1 point sent becomes 3 points received.
  • You then decide how many points from those, if any, to return to them.
  • You can return nothing, some, or all points.
Example:
  • If they send all 3 points, you will receive 9 points.
  • If you return half of the 9 points, you and the other participant will both earn 4.5 points, and will both have more than you started with.
  • If you return nothing, they will earn 9 points, while you will earn nothing.
Your bonus will be the points you keep, and the bonus of the other participant will be the points you return.
