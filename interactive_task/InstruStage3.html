Interaction with a participant from another study

Now, you are matched with a new participant from a different study. We refer to this participant as "Participant C". Participant C will see only one of your randomly selected die rolls, as well as the number you reported for that roll.

{{ if treatment == 'TG' }} Just like in stage 2, {{ endif }} based on this information, after learning how many times you reported the correct number, they will have the opportunity to send you up to 3 points. Every point they send is tripled by the computer: 1 point sent becomes 3 points received. Then, you decide how many points from those, if any, to return to this other participant. You can return nothing, some, or all points.

Example:
  • If they send all 3 points, you will receive 9 points.
  • If you return half of the 9 points, you and Participant C will both earn 4.5 points, and will both have more than you started with.
  • If you return nothing, they will earn 9 points, while you will earn nothing.
Your bonus will be the points you keep, and the bonus of Participant C will be the points you return.
