In stage 3, a new participant from a different study will see only one of your die rolls and the number you reported to us in the first stage. Based on this, the other participant will decide how many points, if any, they wish to send to you.

Remember:
  • The other participant starts with 3 points.
  • The other participant then chooses how many points, if any, to send to you.
  • Any point sent to you is tripled: for every 1 point the other participant sends you, you will receive 3 points.
  • You then choose how many points, if any, to return to the other participant.
  • You can return nothing, any intermediate amount, or everything.

If the other participant sends all 3 points, you will receive 9 points. If you return half of the 9 points, you and the other participant will both earn 4.5 points, and will both have more than the other participant started with. But if you return nothing, you will earn 9 points, while the Sender will earn nothing. So, the other participant can gain points or lose points by sending you points, depending on how much you return.
